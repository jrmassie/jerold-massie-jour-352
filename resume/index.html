About Me

I am an up-and-coming multimedia journalist who loves telling unique and interesting stories in new and engaging ways.

I began my foyer into journalism at the end of my time in community college before interning at a local news website in Southern Maryland. I was offered a job at the end of the summer and decided to take a year off of school and gained valuable experience. I covered government meetings, sports, courts, crime, elections and more.

I have multiple years of experience writing articles and producing video and audio content. I also have experience with photojournalism and livestreaming production.

After my time in local news, I decided to finish my degree at the University of Maryland's Merrill College of Journalism. I will graduate in the spring of 2022 with a BA in Multimedia Journalism and a Tech Entrepreneurship Minor.

In college, I created two of my favorite pieces of journalism in "The Perfect Crime" and "Dueling Monuments." Both are audio stories that cover topics I became more and more intrigued with as I interviewed and read more about them. They both tell unique stories on popular topics. I adore them because they are the kinds of stories that inspired me to become a journalist.

Youth Football Embezzlement

In 2018 I broke the news of a youth sports embezzlement scheme. I made contacts with a variety of parents, the former treasurer, and the president and vice president of the organization. When charges came a few months later, I used those contacts and the ones I had in the local police to stay ahead of competitors in my coverage and provide in depth coverage.

Bears Spotted in SoMD

In the summer of 2019, two black bears wandered down to Southern Maryland, causing a stir in the local community. The two bears were far from their homes in Western Maryland and I spoke with a black bear expert to find out why they were there and if we should be concerned.
